Leamington Geeks

A free, friendly meet-up in Leamington Spa for
geeks

Join us on the last Tuesday of the month from 7:30pm until late.
 

 

Where?

Find us at The White Horse in Leamington Spa.
We are usually at the front of the bar around the corner. It's a good idea to at mention us on Twitter, if you are coming for the first time.

Calendar feed: iCal

We post reminders about the next event on our Twitter: follow Leamington Geeks on twitter.

Getting Here

Bus

Stagecoach and National Express operate services to from Kenilworth, Warwick & Coventry to Leamington. The upper parade stop is the closest one to the White Horse.

Train

Leamington Spa train station is about 15 mins walk from the White Horse.

Car

The best places to park are on Clarendon Avenue outside the pub or Beauchamp Road.
